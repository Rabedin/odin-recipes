Chicken and rice

Simple chicken and rice that can be easily adjusted to fit your macros

  • 200g Chicken (raw weight)
  • 1 Cup Rice (cooked)
  • Olive oil
  • 1/2tsp pink himalayan salt
  • black pepper to taste
  • paprika to taste
  • garlic powder to taste
  • onion powder to taste
  1. Prep 1/2 cup of uncooked rice in your rice cooker
  2. Chop your chicken to your preference, I liked to do roughly 1 inch cubes
  3. Throw it into your large frying pan with all the seasonings
  4. Pour olive oil over it all, don't go overboard
  5. Mix the seasonings and chicken well so that all pieces are evenly covered
  6. Set the burner to medium-high heat and cook it until 165 degrees farenheight at the center, or until it looks cooked enough
  7. Once both the chicken and rice are cooked, put them together in a bowl and enjoy
